QR Stand Designer Pro

Create stunning acrylic QR code stands for your restaurant, café, or retail store. Customise text, colours, brand logo, and QR code – then download a high‑resolution print file.

Frequently Asked Questions

What file format is the download?
The tool exports a high‑resolution PNG image (up to 300 DPI) that is ready for professional printing. You can also screenshot or use the image directly for digital displays.
Can I generate QR codes for WhatsApp, UPI, or Google reviews?
Yes – simply paste your WhatsApp link, UPI payment URL, or Google review link into the “Destination URL” field. The QR code will update automatically.
Which dimensions are best for a table stand?
The “Standard (A6)” size (4.1" × 5.8") is the most common for acrylic table stands. For larger menus, choose the “Large Menu (A5)” size.
How do I add my company logo?
Use the “Brand Logo” upload button. You can choose between “Inline” (logo inside the design) or “Swiggy Pop‑up” (a circular floating logo).
Can I use this design for social media icons (Facebook, Instagram)?
Currently the tool focuses on one primary QR code. However, you can add social media handles in the subtitle text. For multi‑QR designs, consider our custom service.
