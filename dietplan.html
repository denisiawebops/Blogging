General Guidelines

  • Follow the portions: The servings shown in the recipes are automatically tailored to your weight!
  • Ghee: Don't skip the ghee. It aids digestion and provides essential postpartum warmth.
  • Hydration: Drink warm Ajwain (carom) or Jeera (cumin) water throughout the day.
  • Avoid: Raw salads, heavy masalas, chillies, and strictly avoid cold foods/beverages.
