Advanced Prompt Engineering Frameworks for Operations

From Doing to Directing

In the corporate world, the biggest bottleneck is always human bandwidth. Operations managers spend 80% of their time formatting reports and writing scope documents. By mastering prompt frameworks, you transition from "doing the work" to "directing the intelligence."

The R-C-T-O Framework

  • Role (Persona): "Act as a Senior UX Researcher..."
  • Context (Environment): "We are analyzing a dataset of 5,000 tickets..."
  • Task (Action): "Identify the top 3 recurring friction points..."
  • Output (Format): "Format the response as a markdown table."
