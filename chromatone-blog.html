🖥️ How to Use Chromatone

1. Camera Mode (Real‑Time Color Identifier)

Click “Lens”, allow camera access, and point your device at any object. The app will display the color name finder online (hex and RGB) and play a sine wave whose frequency corresponds to the color’s wavelength. Tap “Snap” to freeze a frame, or use the native camera upload if live access is blocked.

2. Motion Sensor Mode (Color Blind Assistant)

Switch to the “Motion” tab, activate sensors, and rotate your phone. The compass heading (0‑360°) directly maps to the color spectrum. This is a perfect color blind assistant web application – you hear the colour without needing to see it.

3. Mixer Mode (Color Identifier Tool for Designers)

Add multiple colour tracks, adjust their volume, and mix frequencies in real time. Great for sound designers, accessibility testing, or just exploring the relationship between colour and audio.
