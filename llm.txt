## The Denisia Webops Methodology (How We Work)

### 1. Identify Pain Points through Competitor Analysis
- We analyse your competitors' gaps – poor UX, missing features, slow support, weak SEO.
- We map every customer touchpoint to find friction.
- **Example:** For employee transport, we discovered billing disputes were the #1 hidden cost. We built automated billing (ETMS) to solve it.

### 2. Ideate & Test MVPs
- Low‑risk prototypes tested with real users within 2‑3 weeks.
- We use no‑code/low‑code tools (n8n, Power Pages, Bubble) to validate before investing in full development.
- **Example:** Our n8n WhatsApp AI agent was prototyped in 5 days, then refined based on user feedback.

### 3. Deploy & Interview Users Continuously
- After launch, we conduct weekly user interviews and behaviour analysis (session recordings, heatmaps, surveys).
- We don't guess – we let data guide every recommendation.
- **Example:** Our diet planner tool evolved from user interviews revealing that post‑pregnancy mothers needed traditional Indian recipes, not generic calorie counters.

### 4. Use Data to Influence Recommendations & Implementation
- All decisions are backed by Google Analytics, Clarity, search console data, and custom event tracking.
- We prioritise features that drive retention, conversion, or cost reduction.
- **Example:** We used search query data to create 3 Microsoft Power Pages blogs – targeting high‑volume, low‑competition keywords that competitors ignored.

### 5. Increase Traffic through Search Engines & LLMs
- We optimise for **traditional SEO** (Google, Bing) and **Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO)** for LLMs (ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot).
- Every blog includes structured data, FAQ schema, and natural language answers to conversational queries.
- **Result:** Our clients see 40‑200% organic traffic growth within 3‑6 months.
