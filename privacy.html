Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how Denisia Webops Agency ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you visit our website www.denisiawebops.org (the "Site") or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: name, email address, phone number, company name – when you fill out our contact form or request a consultation.
  • Usage Data: automatically collected via Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity – includes IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs.
  • Newsletter Data: email address if you subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Files Uploaded: when you use our Live Code Preview & Scanner, any HTML/CSS/JS files you upload are processed locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit those files to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request (web development, SEO, app development, etc.).
  • Improve our website, tools, and user experience (analytics).
  • Send periodic emails (newsletters, service updates) if you have opted in.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies (including Google Analytics and Clarity) to analyse traffic, remember user preferences, and improve our Site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the Site.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., Google Analytics, Tawk.to chat, hosting providers) who assist us in operating the Site, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@denisiawebops.com.

7. Third‑Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third‑party websites (e.g., Unsplash images, affiliate links to AI tools). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Your continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:
