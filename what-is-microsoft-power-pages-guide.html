What is Microsoft Power Pages? The Low‑Code Website Builder You Should Know (2026)

Discover how Microsoft Power Pages helps you create secure, data-driven websites without writing code. Perfect for small business, enterprise portals, and customer self-service.

❓ So, What is Microsoft Power Pages?

Microsoft Power Pages is a low-code, software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform for building external-facing business websites. It’s part of the Microsoft Power Platform (alongside Power BI, Power Apps, and Power Automate). Unlike traditional website builders, Power Pages connects directly to Microsoft Dataverse, allowing you to display and collect data from your existing business systems.

If you’ve searched “what is microsoft power pages” or “what are power pages”, you’re in the right place. Power Pages replaces the old “Power Apps Portals” with a more intuitive design studio, templates, and built-in security.

🧩 Key Features That Make Power Pages Stand Out

  • Low-code designer – Drag, drop, and style pages without HTML/CSS knowledge.
  • Dataverse integration – Show data from tables, create forms, and manage permissions.
  • Built-in authentication – Azure AD, local logins, OAuth, or social providers.
  • Responsive themes – Mobile-ready templates out of the box.
  • Visual styling – Custom CSS and JavaScript also supported for developers.
  • SEO tools – Sitemaps, meta tags, and URL customization.
  • Multilingual support – Publish content in dozens of languages.

⚙️ How is Power Pages Different from Other Microsoft Tools?

Many users confuse Power Pages with Power Apps or SharePoint. Here’s a quick breakdown:

  • Power Apps – Build internal apps (canvas or model-driven). Not for public websites.
  • SharePoint – Internal intranet and document management, not designed for external customer portals.
  • Power Pages – Specifically built for authenticated or anonymous external users (customers, partners, vendors).

So when someone asks “what is power pages website builder microsoft” – it’s Microsoft’s answer to tools like Wix or WordPress but deeply connected to business data.

📊 Real-World Use Cases

🏥 Healthcare: Patient appointment portals, lab results, prescription refills.
🏦 Financial services: Loan application tracking, document uploads, secure messaging.
🛍️ E‑commerce: Vendor registration, order status, return requests.
📋 Government: Permit applications, citizen feedback, service requests.

💰 Pricing and Licensing (India & Global)

Power Pages uses a pay-as-you-go model based on authenticated user logins (or page views for anonymous). Approximate costs:

  • Anonymous users – ₹2,500 per 100,000 page views / month.
  • Authenticated users – ₹11,500 per 100 users / month (includes Dataverse capacity).
  • Trial – 30 days free with 1 GB Dataverse storage.

For Indian SMBs, Power Pages can be cheaper than hiring a developer for a custom portal. Compare with traditional website builders: WordPress hosting + plugins + security easily exceeds ₹15,000/month.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions about Microsoft Power Pages

Is Microsoft Power Pages free?
It offers a free 30-day trial. After that, it’s consumption-based starting from ~₹2,500/month for anonymous page views.
Do I need to know coding to use Power Pages?
No – the design studio is drag-and-drop. However, developers can extend with custom HTML/CSS/JS.
What is the difference between Power Pages and Power Apps portals?
Power Pages is the rebranded and improved version of Power Apps Portals (launched 2022). It has a new designer, better performance, and modern templates.
Can I host a blog on Power Pages?
Yes, but it’s not optimized for frequent blog posts. Better for business portals, knowledge bases, and self-service apps.
How secure is Microsoft Power Pages?
Very secure – it runs on Azure infrastructure with TLS 1.2+, Azure AD integration, row-level security, and DDoS protection.
