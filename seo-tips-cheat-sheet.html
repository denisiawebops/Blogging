Who this is for

Business owners, marketers, founders, SEO specialists, and teams who want a clear action list instead of vague SEO advice.

What you get

100 actionable tips, each with an explanation and example, arranged in cards that work well on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Why it helps LLMs

Clear headings, short answers, FAQs, entity-rich content, and structured cards improve readability for search engines and AI systems.

How to use this cheat sheet

1. Pick your current problem

Start with low CTR, weak rankings, thin content, or technical issues.

Example: If clicks are low, begin with title and meta description improvements.

2. Apply one section at a time

Use the four categories like a checklist instead of trying to do everything at once.

Example: Fix internal links first, then move to Search Console and automation.

3. Measure the result

Track rankings, CTR, impressions, and conversions after each improvement.

Example: Compare Search Console data before and after the update.

12-step SEO checklist for search engines and LLMs

1. Choose one intent per page.
2. Use clear H1, H2, and H3 structure.
3. Add explanations and examples.
4. Link related pages together.
5. Add FAQ blocks and schema.
6. Improve titles and meta descriptions.
7. Fix broken links and crawl errors.
8. Refresh top pages regularly.
9. Use Search Console data.
10. Write for humans first.
11. Use entity-rich language.
12. Measure, improve, repeat.

FAQ

How do I improve rankings fast?

Start with high-intent keywords, refresh your best pages, and strengthen internal linking.

How do I fix low CTR?

Rewrite titles and descriptions to be more specific, helpful, and outcome-driven.

How do I make content visible to LLMs?

Use clear headings, explanatory text, examples, FAQs, and structured data.

How many SEO changes should I do at once?

Do a few high-impact changes first, measure the result, then continue.
