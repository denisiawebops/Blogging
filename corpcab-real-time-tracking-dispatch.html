CorpCab: Real‑Time Employee Tracking & Automated Dispatch System

Stop wondering where your corporate cabs are. CorpCab (corporate cab) software gives you live GPS tracking, automated dispatch, and a driver app – reducing idle time and improving employee safety.

📍 What is CorpCab?

CorpCab (short for corporate cab) is a dedicated employee transport solution that focuses on real‑time visibility and intelligent dispatching. Unlike generic taxi apps, CorpCab is built for recurring shift‑based commutes, with features like:

  • Live map view of all company vehicles.
  • Automated cab assignment based on employee location and shift timing.
  • Driver mobile app with turn‑by‑turn navigation.
  • Employee app with ETA and SOS button.

⚙️ How Automated Dispatch Works

Traditional dispatching relies on radio calls or WhatsApp groups – slow and error‑prone. CorpCab’s automated dispatch engine:

  • Receives a trip request (scheduled or on‑demand).
  • Checks nearby available vehicles using real‑time GPS.
  • Assigns the closest, most suitable cab based on capacity and route.
  • Sends push notification to driver and passenger.

This reduces wait times by up to 40% and maximises fleet utilisation.

📱 Employee Shuttle Tracking App – Peace of Mind

Safety is the #1 concern for night‑shift employees, especially women. CorpCab includes a white‑label employee shuttle tracking app that allows staff to:

  • See their assigned cab on a live map.
  • Get a 2‑minute arrival alert.
  • Share live trip status with family members.
  • Trigger an SOS that notifies transport managers and local security.

Companies using CorpCab have reported a 95% reduction in safety‑related anxiety among employees.

❓ FAQ – CorpCab & Automated Dispatch

What is the difference between CorpCab and a regular taxi app?
CorpCab is designed for corporate fleets – it handles recurring shift rosters, multiple pickups/drop‑offs, company billing, and employee safety features not found in consumer apps like Uber.
Does CorpCab include a driver app?
Yes. Drivers get a dedicated app with navigation, trip acceptance, proof‑of‑pickup, and digital log sheets.
Can employees track their cab in real time?
Absolutely. The employee app shows live location, ETA, and driver contact (with number masking).
